## Quick Start: 5 Minutes to Your First Investment Insight

Follow these steps to get started with Bloom and make your first investment research in just 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bloom">
    Go to [investwithbloom.com](https://investwithbloom.com) or download the mobile app from the App Store or Google Play
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create an account with your email address—no credit card required for the free trial
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile (Optional)">
    Add your investment goals and experience level to personalize your experience
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Add Stocks to Your Watchlist

<Frame caption="Watch: Adding Your First Stocks">
  <video
    controls
    style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '8px' }}
  >
    <source src="/assets/videos/Bloom Product Demo.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for Stocks">
    Use the search bar to find stocks you own or want to research (e.g., "Apple", "AAPL")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to Watchlist">
    Click the "+" button to add stocks to your default watchlist
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Custom Lists (Optional)">
    Organize stocks into themed watchlists:
    - **Core Holdings:** Long-term investments
    - **Growth Stocks:** High-growth opportunities
    - **Research Ideas:** Stocks under consideration
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Notifications (Mobile)">
    Turn on push notifications for key events like earnings and analyst ratings
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Tip:** Start with 10-15 stocks you already know (stocks you own or follow). You can always add more later.
</Info>

## Step 3: Ask Your First Investment Question

<Frame caption="Watch: Your First AI Chat">
  <video
    controls
    style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '8px' }}
  >
    <source src="/assets/videos/aapl rotato.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Chat Icon">
    Navigate to the AI Assistant (chat icon in the navigation)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a Question">
    Try these starter questions:

    - "Is Apple a good buy right now?"
    - "What are the risks in the tech sector?"
    - "Find dividend stocks with yields above 4%"
    - "Analyze my portfolio: 40% AAPL, 30% GOOGL, 30% MSFT"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch the AI Research">
    See in real-time as the AI:
    - Fetches financial data
    - Analyzes earnings transcripts
    - Reviews technical indicators
    - Searches for relevant news
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the Answer">
    Get comprehensive analysis with:
    - Buy/Hold/Sell recommendation
    - Supporting data and reasoning
    - Key risks and catalysts
    - Next steps
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Ask follow-up questions to dig deeper. The AI remembers your conversation context.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Explore Daily Investment Ideas

<Frame caption="Watch: Discovering Investment Ideas">
  <video
    controls
    style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '8px' }}
  >
    <source src="/assets/videos/Bloom Collections.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Collections">
    Click on "Collections" or "Daily Ideas" in the menu
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Stock Picks">
    Explore curated collections updated daily:
    - **Top Momentum Stocks:** High relative strength
    - **Undervalued Gems:** Low P/E with strong fundamentals
    - **Dividend Aristocrats:** Reliable income
    - **AI & Innovation Plays:** Cutting-edge technology
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read AI Reasoning">
    Click any stock to see detailed investment thesis explaining why it's featured
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save to Watchlist">
    Add interesting stocks to your watchlist for tracking and further research
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Set Up Portfolio Analysis

<Frame caption="Watch: Portfolio Analysis Walkthrough">
  <video
    controls
    style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '8px' }}
  >
    <source src="/assets/videos/Bloom Portfolio Backtest.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Portfolio">
    Navigate to "Portfolios" and click "Create New Portfolio"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Holdings">
    Enter stock symbols and allocation percentages:

    **Example Portfolio:**
    - AAPL: 25%
    - GOOGL: 20%
    - MSFT: 20%
    - NVDA: 15%
    - AMZN: 20%
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask AI for Analysis">
    Use the AI Assistant: "Analyze my portfolio for risks and opportunities"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Recommendations">
    Get insights on:
    - Sector concentration and diversification
    - Correlation between holdings
    - Risk assessment
    - Rebalancing suggestions
  </Step>

  <Step title="Backtest Performance (Optional)">
    See how your portfolio would have performed historically
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Bloom free to use?" icon="dollar-sign">
    Bloom offers a free trial with limited features. Premium plans start at $15/month for full access to:
    - Unlimited AI Assistant queries
    - All Daily Investment Ideas collections
    - Advanced portfolio analysis and backtesting
    - Push notifications and email digests
    - Priority support

    [See pricing details](https://investwithbloom.com/pricing)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Bloom for trading?" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    **No.** Bloom is a **research platform**, not a trading platform.

    We help you make better investment decisions through:
    - AI-powered research and analysis
    - Data aggregation and insights
    - Portfolio optimization suggestions

    You'll need a separate brokerage account (Robinhood, Fidelity, Schwab, etc.) to execute trades.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What data sources does Bloom use?" icon="database">
    Bloom aggregates data from 20+ premium sources:

    **Financial Data:**
    - AlphaVantage (quotes, fundamentals, earnings)
    - IEX Cloud (insider trading, backup data)
    - EOD Historical Data (ETF holdings, international)

    **AI Models:**
    - OpenAI GPT-4o (analysis, reasoning)
    - Anthropic Claude (document analysis)
    - Groq/Cerebras (high-speed inference)

    **News & Sentiment:**
    - Benzinga (news, "Why It's Moving")
    - Perplexity (real-time web search)

    [See full list →](/guides/platform/data-sources)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?" icon="shield">
    **Yes.** Bloom takes security seriously:

    - **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
    - **Authentication:** Secure password hashing with bcrypt
    - **Privacy:** We never sell your data or share it with third parties
    - **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant

    Your watchlists, portfolios, and queries are private and never used to train AI models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Bloom on multiple devices?" icon="devices">
    **Yes!** Bloom syncs seamlessly across:
    - Web app (desktop/laptop)
    - iOS app (iPhone/iPad)
    - Android app (phone/tablet)

    Add a stock on your phone, see it instantly on your laptop. All watchlists, portfolios, and preferences sync in real-time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer investment advice?" icon="circle-info">
    **No.** Bloom provides research tools and AI-powered insights, but we do not provide personalized investment advice.

    Our AI Assistant offers:
    - Data-driven analysis
    - Investment research
    - Risk assessments
    - Educational content

    **We do not:**
    - Provide fiduciary advice
    - Make specific buy/sell recommendations for your situation
    - Manage investments on your behalf

    Always consult with a licensed financial advisor for personalized investment advice.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
