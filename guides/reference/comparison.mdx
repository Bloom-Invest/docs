## Who Should Use Bloom?

### ✅ Perfect For:

- **Individual investors** wanting institutional-quality research
- **Busy professionals** with limited time (15-30 min/day)
- **Active traders** needing real-time technical analysis
- **Portfolio managers** seeking AI-powered optimization
- **Long-term investors** focused on diversification and risk
- **Power users** wanting MCP integration with Claude Desktop

### ⚠️ Not Ideal For:

- **Professional traders** needing Level 2 data and trading desk tools (use Bloomberg)
- **Fixed income investors** focused on bonds and derivatives (use Bloomberg/FactSet)
- **International traders** needing 24/7 global coverage (use Bloomberg)
- **Completely free** users unwilling to pay $15/month (use Yahoo Finance)
- **Human advice seekers** preferring advisors over AI (use financial advisor)
