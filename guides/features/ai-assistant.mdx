## Example Workflows

### Workflow 1: Deep Stock Research

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask a research question">
    **User:** "Should I invest in Nvidia at current prices?"
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI gathers fundamental data">
    Calls `get_symbols_metadata(["NVDA"])` to fetch:
    - Current price: $875
    - P/E ratio: 45
    - Revenue growth: 122% YoY
    - Market cap: $2.1T
    - AI rating: Strong Buy
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI analyzes earnings">
    Calls `analyze_earnings("NVDA")` to review:
    - Earnings growth trend: +427% YoY
    - Beat estimates by 18% last quarter
    - Guidance: Strong data center demand
    - EPS forecast: $25-27 for next year
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI checks technicals">
    Calls `analyze_technicals("NVDA")` to assess:
    - RSI: 62 (neutral, not overbought)
    - Price above 50/200 SMA (bullish trend)
    - Volume: Higher than average
    - Support level: $850
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI searches transcripts">
    Calls `search_transcript("NVDA", ["guidance", "data center"])` to find:
    - "Data center revenue up 427% YoY"
    - "AI accelerator demand exceeds supply"
    - "Blackwell chip seeing strong early orders"
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI provides recommendation">
    **Bloom Agent Response:**

    **Rating: BUY**

    **Reasoning:**
    - Strong fundamental growth (122% revenue, 427% earnings)
    - Positive technical setup (above key moving averages, healthy RSI)
    - Bullish management guidance on AI accelerators
    - Valuation reasonable given growth (PEG ratio: 0.4)

    **Risks:**
    - High valuation multiples (P/E: 45)
    - Concentration risk in data center segment
    - Competition from AMD and custom chips

    **Recommendation:** Consider accumulating on dips to $850 support
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Workflow 2: Portfolio Analysis

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit portfolio for review">
    **User:** "Review my portfolio: 40% AAPL, 30% GOOGL, 20% MSFT, 10% TSLA"
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI analyzes allocation">
    Calls `analyze_portfolio` with holdings dictionary:
    - Detects 100% concentration in tech sector
    - Identifies high correlation (avg: 0.72)
    - Calculates sector exposure and risk metrics
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI identifies risks">
    **Key Findings:**
    - **Sector Concentration:** 100% technology (high risk)
    - **Correlation:** AAPL-MSFT correlation: 0.85 (move together)
    - **Volatility:** Portfolio beta: 1.35 (35% more volatile than market)
    - **Diversification Score:** 3/10 (poor)
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI provides recommendations">
    **Rebalancing Suggestions:**

    **Reduce Tech Exposure (to 60-70%):**
    - Trim AAPL to 25% (-15%)
    - Trim GOOGL to 20% (-10%)
    - Hold MSFT at 20%
    - Consider selling TSLA (10%)

    **Add Defensive Sectors (30-40%):**
    - Healthcare: 15% (JNJ, UNH)
    - Utilities: 10% (NEE, DUK)
    - Consumer Staples: 10% (PG, KO)

    **Expected Impact:**
    - Reduce portfolio beta to ~1.1
    - Lower correlation to 0.5
    - Improve diversification score to 7/10
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Effective Prompts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Specific" icon="bullseye">
    **Good:** "Should I buy Microsoft now given their cloud growth?"

    **Better:** "Analyze MSFT considering Azure growth, AI products, and valuation vs historical P/E"

    Specificity helps the AI understand exactly what aspects to focus on.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ask Follow-Up Questions" icon="comments">
    The AI remembers context from your conversation. Build on previous answers:

    - "What are the main risks?"
    - "How does this compare to its competitors?"
    - "What's the best entry price?"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Request Specific Formats" icon="list">
    You can ask for structured responses:

    - "Give me a bull and bear case for Tesla"
    - "Create a 5-point checklist for evaluating this stock"
    - "Compare these 3 stocks in a table"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Combine Multiple Analyses" icon="layer-group">
    The AI can handle complex multi-part questions:

    - "Analyze Apple's fundamentals, then check if the technicals support buying now"
    - "Review my portfolio and suggest 3 defensive stocks to add"
    - "Find high-growth healthcare stocks and analyze the top 3"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
